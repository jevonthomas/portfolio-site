About Me

Despite having a solid career lined up for myself as a business leader, I could not fight my desire to become a Web Developer. Throughout my education and career progression, I had always been drawn to some form of design or development activities. Whether it was using HTML to customize my layouts on Myspace and BlackPlanet, manipulating photos or creating graphics in Photoshop, or creating animations in Flash, I would normally find myself spending hours on one of these activities during my free time.

After reflecting on my life and realizing this pattern of behavior, I determined that I was born to be a Web Developer. So I ended my career as a business manager and joined Nashville Software School's Web Development Boot Camp to acheive this goal, and now I am ready to begin my journey as a Front End Web Developer.

This is my capstone project for the Front-End portion of my training at NSS.
